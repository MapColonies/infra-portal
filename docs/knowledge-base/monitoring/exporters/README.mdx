**Exporters** are specialized, lightweight adapters that bridge the gap between third-party systems and Prometheus! 🌉

Since not every application natively speaks the Prometheus metrics format, exporters act as translators. They fetch data from external systems, translate it on the fly, and serve it up on a standard `/metrics` endpoint for Prometheus to scrape.

## 🛠️ Our Exporter Stack

We have curated a powerful stack of exporters to give you deep visibility into our infrastructure, databases, and managed services—all without you needing to write custom metric-gathering code! 🚀

* 🌍 **[Blackbox Exporter](./blackbox-exporter.mdx):** Actively probes external and internal HTTP/HTTPS endpoints to monitor uptime, response times, and general availability.
* 🐘 **[Postgres Exporter](./postgres-exporter.mdx):** Gathers deep performance, connection, and health metrics directly from our central PostgreSQL database.
* 🛡️ **[Cert Exporter](./cert-exporter.mdx):** Constantly monitors TLS certificate expiration dates across the cluster to prevent unexpected outages.
* ☸️ **[Kube State Metrics (KSM)](./kube-state-metrics.mdx):** Listens to the Kubernetes API to generate high-fidelity metrics about the state of our deployments, pods, allocations, and nodes.
* ⚡ **[Redis Exporter](./redis-exporter.mdx):** Exposes memory, connection, and performance metrics for both central and application-specific Redis instances.
