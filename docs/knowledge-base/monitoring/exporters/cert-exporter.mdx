The Cert Exporter automatically monitors the expiration dates of TLS certificates across our environment. 
By exposing certificate validity as Prometheus metrics, it ensures we are proactively alerted before any certificates expire, preventing unexpected service downtime.

## Scanning and Discovery

The Cert Exporter is configured by the infrastructure team to scan designated namespaces across the cluster. 
While it monitors these areas continuously, certain resources—specifically raw Kubernetes Secrets containing 
TLS certificates—require explicit tagging to be recognized and parsed by the exporter's discovery engine.
