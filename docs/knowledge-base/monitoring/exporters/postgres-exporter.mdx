The **Postgres Exporter** is your window into the health and performance of our central PostgreSQL database. 🐘📊

## 🔭 Monitoring Scope

We rely on a single, robust, centrally managed PostgreSQL instance for the environment. 

The infrastructure team configures the Postgres Exporter globally. It connects directly to this database and effortlessly exposes standard performance metrics—like active connections, transaction rates, and buffer behavior—straight to Prometheus. 

By sticking to these standard, built-in metrics, we ensure a highly consistent baseline for our database observability. We get all the insights we need, completely avoiding the overhead of writing and maintaining custom queries! 🚀
