Grafana Tempo is our highly efficient, distributed tracing backend. 
It is designed to store and query trace data at high throughput without requiring a complex index, making it perfect for tracking requests across our services. 

## Architecture & Data Flow

* **Ingestion:** Tempo does not collect traces directly from applications. Instead, it receives all trace data exclusively from our **OpenTelemetry Collector**.
* **Storage:** We use **S3** as the backend object storage for Tempo. This allows us to store massive amounts of trace data reliably and cost-effectively.
* **Visualization:** Tempo is natively integrated into Grafana. Once the OpenTelemetry Collector sends traces to Tempo, 
you can view, query, and analyze these traces seamlessly within the Grafana UI.
