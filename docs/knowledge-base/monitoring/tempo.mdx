# ⏱️ Grafana Tempo

**Grafana Tempo** is our lightning-fast, highly distributed tracing backend! 🚀

It is precision-engineered to ingest, store, and query massive amounts of trace data at incredible throughput *without* requiring a bloated, complex index. This makes it the perfect tool for tracking requests jumping across our microservices!

## 🏗️ Architecture & Data Flow

* 📥 **Ingestion:** Tempo does not scrape or collect traces directly from your applications! Instead, it receives a perfectly clean, processed stream of trace data exclusively from our **OpenTelemetry Collector**.
* 💾 **Storage:** We back Tempo entirely with **S3** object storage. This lets us stash terabytes of trace data incredibly reliably and cost-effectively!
* 📊 **Visualization:** Tempo has no UI of its own; it is natively integrated straight into Grafana! The second traces hit Tempo, you can instantly query, slice, and analyze them seamlessly within the Grafana interface.
