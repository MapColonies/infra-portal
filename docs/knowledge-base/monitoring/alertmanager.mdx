# 🚨 Alertmanager

You can access Libot's Alertmanager at [https://alertmanager.mapcolonies.net](https://alertmanager.mapcolonies.net).

:::warning 🚧 Upcoming Migration
We are actively planning a massive migration from Alertmanager to native **Grafana Alerts**! 
Alertmanager is still running the show for now, but be aware that this workflow will be leveling up in the near future.
:::

Alertmanager is the central notification dispatcher for our entire observability stack. 
Instead of spamming you with a raw notification every single time a metric spikes, Alertmanager intelligently groups, deduplicates, and routes alerts directly to the correct communication channels. 🤫

![Alertmanager home screen](/img/monitoring/alertmanager/alertmanager-homescreen.png)

## 🗂️ Alert Categorization

To keep our notifications sane and actionable, alerts are strictly categorized:
* 🔥 **Severity:** Classified as `critical`, `warning`, or `info`.
* 🌍 **Environment:** Tagged with `production`, `staging`, or `development` so you know the exact context.
* 🏷️ **Service Labels:** We use tags like `service` and `team` to pinpoint exactly which component is misbehaving.

## 🔀 Routing Destinations

Alertmanager is wired up to blast alerts to multiple platforms based on their tags:
* **Slack**
* **Classified Chat Systems**
* **Email**
