Alertmanager is the notification hub for our observability stack. It is responsible for handling and routing all alerts generated by Prometheus. 
Instead of firing off a raw notification for every single metric spike, Alertmanager manages these alerts by grouping them, 
deduplicating them, and routing them to the correct communication channels.

![Alertmanager home screen](/img/monitoring/alertmanager/alertmanager-homescreen.png)

## Alert Categorization

To keep notifications organized and actionable, our alerts are categorized by:
* **Severity:** Usually classified as `critical`, `warning`, or `info`.
* **Environment:** Tagged with `production`, `staging`, or `development` to indicate context.
* **Service Labels:** Specific tags like `service` and `team` to pinpoint exactly which component is failing.

## Routing Destinations

Alertmanager is configured to route alerts to several different platforms based on their tags, including:
* Slack
* Classified chat systems
* Email
