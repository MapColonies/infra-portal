This guide covers how to send your application traces to our observability stack. For general concepts and supported languages, refer to the [official OpenTelemetry documentation](https://opentelemetry.io/docs/collector/).

## Sending Traces

Your services should be configured to send distributed traces exclusively to the **External OpenTelemetry Collector**.

* **Service Endpoint:** `http://infra-otel`

Make sure you configure the appropriate port:
* 4317 - grpc
* 4318 - http

## Trace Filtering

You do not need to manually filter out health checks or Prometheus scrape endpoints (like `/metrics` or `/health`) within your application's telemetry configuration. 

Our **Internal Collector** is configured to automatically drop spans related to these URLs:
* `health`
* `liveness`
* `metrics`

This ensures our tracing backend remains uncluttered and cost-effective, without requiring duplicate configuration across every microservice.
