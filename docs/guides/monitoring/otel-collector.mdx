# 📡 OpenTelemetry Collector Guides

Time to get your traces flowing! This guide will show you exactly how to hook your applications into our observability stack. 

*(Curious about supported languages? See the [official OpenTelemetry documentation](https://opentelemetry.io/docs/collector/).)*

## 📤 Sending Traces

To get your distributed traces recorded, your services must be configured to point exclusively to the **External OpenTelemetry Collector**.

* 🌐 **Service Endpoint:** `http://infra-otel`

Just pick the port that matches your protocol:
* **4317** - gRPC
* **4318** - HTTP

## 🧹 Automatic Trace Filtering (We do it for you!)

Great news: You do **not** need to write messy code to filter out noisy health checks or Prometheus scrape endpoints (like `/metrics` or `/health`) in your app! 🎉

Our **Internal Collector** is already configured to automatically drop spans hitting these common URLs:
* `health`
* `liveness`
* `metrics`

Why? This keeps our tracing backend fast, uncluttered, and cost-effective, saving you from writing duplicate filtering logic across all your microservices! 🚀
