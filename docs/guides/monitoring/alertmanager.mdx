# 🚨 Alertmanager Guides

Welcome! This guide covers exactly how you, as a developer, will interact with Alertmanager to keep tabs on your services. 

*(Want the deep technical dive? Check out the [official Alertmanager documentation](https://prometheus.io/docs/alerting/latest/alertmanager/).)*

## 👀 Viewing and Managing Active Alerts

While you cannot edit global routing rules, you have full power to view and manage active alerts directly through the UI!

1. Jump into [Alertmanager](https://alertmanager.mapcolonies.net).
2. Check the main dashboard to see all currently firing alerts neatly grouped by their labels.
3. 🤫 **Silencing:** Is an alert already a known issue your team is fixing? Click **Silence** to temporarily mute notifications for that alert group. Your teammates will thank you for reducing the alert fatigue!

![Alertmanager active alert](/img/monitoring/alertmanager/alertmanager-alert.png)

## 🔀 Requesting Routing Changes

:::note 🏗️ Infrastructure Managed
Alert routing (deciding which alerts blast to which Slack channels or emails) is handled globally by the **infra team**.
:::

Currently, developers have **read-only access** to routing configurations. You don't have to worry about configuring routing for your apps!

**Need a change?** If your team spun up a new Slack channel, or you need specific service alerts sent to a new destination, just ping the **infra team** and they will adjust the global configuration for you. 🤝
