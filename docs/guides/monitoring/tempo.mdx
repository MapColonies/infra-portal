# ⏱️ Tempo Tracing

Welcome to Tempo, our high-speed tracing backend! 

*(Want to master TraceQL? Dive into the [official Grafana Tempo documentation](https://grafana.com/docs/tempo/latest/).)*

## 📊 Visualizing Traces in Grafana

Tempo works silently in the background, so all your hands-on trace analysis will happen right inside Grafana.

**Here is how to hunt down a trace:**
1. Open Grafana and hit the **Explore** tab (the compass icon on the left). 🧭
2. Choose **Tempo** from the data source dropdown at the top left.
3. Start exploring! You have two ways to search:
   * 🆔 **Trace ID:** Got an exact Trace ID from an error log? Paste it straight into the search bar to instantly pull up the request waterfall!
   * 🔎 **Search Explorer:** Use the visual builder to hunt for traces based on specific tags, service names, or even slow response durations.

## 🪄 Trace to Logs (The Magic Pivot)

When you are staring at a massive trace waterfall, pay close attention to the individual spans. 

If you see a tiny **document icon** next to a span, click it! 
This will seamlessly pivot you straight into **Loki**, instantly revealing the exact logs generated during that specific microsecond of the request! It is an absolute game-changer for debugging slow or failed requests. 🎯
