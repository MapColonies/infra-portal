# 🔍 Loki How-To Guides

Ready to hunt down some bugs? This guide shows you how to search and analyze logs effortlessly within our environment. 

*(For advanced LogQL tricks, check out the [official Grafana Loki documentation](https://grafana.com/docs/loki/latest/).)*

## 📊 Querying Logs in Grafana

Loki is a backend powerhouse, so it does not have its own UI. Instead, we use Grafana as our magnifying glass!

1. Open Grafana and click the **Explore** tab (the compass icon on the left). 🧭
2. Select **Loki** from the data source dropdown at the top left.
3. Use the **Label filters** to instantly narrow down your hunt (e.g., set `app` = `my-backend-service` or `namespace` = `production`).
4. Smash that **Run query** button to stream your logs! 🚀

## 🪄 Jumping from Traces to Logs (Magic!)

Here is the absolute coolest feature of our stack: seamless integration between **Tempo** (traces) and **Loki** (logs). ✨

Are you staring at a slow or failed request trace in Tempo? 
1. Look closely for a tiny **document icon** right next to the failing span. 
2. **Click it!** 
3. Grafana will instantly split the screen and query Loki for the *exact logs* generated by that specific service during the exact microsecond of that trace! 

It is the ultimate debugging shortcut! 🎯
